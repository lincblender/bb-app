# Whole-of-Government Cloud Framework Brief

Issuer: Department of Digital Services
Opportunity: Whole-of-Government Cloud Framework
Type: RFT

Summary:
TechServe Solutions is considering a response for a multi-year cloud transformation panel. The scope includes migration planning, managed landing zones, cost optimisation, cyber controls, and service transition support for agencies moving off legacy infrastructure.

Delivery expectations:
- Multi-cloud architecture across AWS and Azure
- Edge-compute readiness for regional workloads
- Security accreditation support
- Change management for agency uplift teams
- Transition governance with monthly steering committees

Evaluation signals:
- Capability and prior whole-of-government delivery experience
- Security and compliance posture
- Price competitiveness
- Relationship strength and responsiveness during procurement

Questions for review:
- Is this worth bidding?
- Where are the likely blockers?
- Would a partner improve price coverage without weakening delivery quality?
