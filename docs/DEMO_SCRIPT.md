# BidBlender Demo Script — Golden Path

A step-by-step script to demonstrate BidBlender's opportunity intelligence value without implementing the full AI chat agent. Use these prompts in order to walk through the platform's capabilities.

---

## Demo Persona

**You are:** TechServe Solutions (the bidder) — an Australian IT services provider specialising in government and enterprise digital transformation, cloud migration, and cybersecurity.

**Your capabilities:** Cloud Migration, Cybersecurity, Digital Transformation, Data Analytics, Managed Services.

**Your relationships:** Strong connections into Department of Digital Services, State Health Authority, National Infrastructure Corp. Michael Park has 15 connections into NIC.

---

## Golden Path (15–20 min)

### 1. Discover matching opportunities (2 min)

**Say:** *"What's the latest matching bids with my capabilities?"*

**What to show:**
- Opportunity cards with Fit %, competitive %, Chat and Open buttons
- Chat tagged with multiple opportunities
- Break apart CTA for multi-opp chats

**Demo tip:** Click the Chat icon on one card to create a focused chat. Click Open on another to show the opportunity detail page.

---

### 2. Track movement and updates (2 min)

**Say:** *"What's the movement on Department of Digital Services?"*

**What to show:**
- Addenda, scope changes, contact changes
- Relationship context (e.g. "Michael Park has direct access to the new contact")
- Compare to last bid CTA

---

### 3. Document review and bid-no-bid (3 min)

**Say:** *"Review this doc against my last few bids — is it worth bidding?"*

*(Or upload a PDF to trigger the same flow.)*

**What to show:**
- Similar vs different analysis
- Partnership recommendation
- Bid-no-bid summary CTA

---

### 4. Competitive intelligence (2 min)

**Say:** *"Run a full competitive intelligence analysis on Defence Cyber Uplift"*

**What to show:**
- Compintel queued message
- Link to Opportunities list
- Open opportunity detail for score breakdown

---

### 5. **Competitor impact** (2 min) — *High-value prompt*

**Say:** Any prompt that includes both **"competitor"** and **"impact"** — e.g. *"What's the competitor impact on our Defence tender?"* or *"Will our competitor's acquisition impact our relationship?"*

**What to show:**
- Staggered reply: Impact assessment → Relationship angle → Recommendation
- M&A risk analysis
- Relationship continuity advice

---

### 6. **Current customer pipeline** (2 min) — *High-value prompt*

**Say:** *"Are any of my current customers doing RFT or EOI this month?"*

**What to show:**
- List of existing customers with active tenders
- Relationship strength into each
- Prioritisation by fit and access

---

### 7. Break apart and archive (2 min)

**From the chat menu (⋮):**
- Show Break apart with named opportunities
- Click one to create a focused chat
- Archive a chat, then toggle "Show archived" in the filter menu

---

### 8. Context panel and opportunity detail (2 min)

- Collapse/expand the context panel
- Click section icons to open and emphasise sections
- **Network section** — LinkedIn connections, follows (Provider vs competitor), Network optimism %
- Open an opportunity from a card → show Back to Opps, Chat button
- Click Chat to open or create a chat for that opportunity

---

## Curated Prompt Library

Prompts for demo use and future AI training. Grouped by intent.

### Discovery & matching
- What's the latest matching bids with my capabilities?
- What opportunities match my cloud and cybersecurity profile?
- Show me tenders from TenderLink that fit our capabilities
- What's new on the tender boards this week?

### Movement & updates
- What's the movement on Department of Digital Services?
- What's changed in the last 48 hours?
- Any addenda to the Whole-of-Government Cloud Framework?
- Has the NIC Digital Transformation Program been updated?

### Document review & bid-no-bid
- Review this doc against my last few bids — is it worth bidding?
- Compare this RFT to our State Govt Cloud Migration win
- What's different about this evaluation criteria vs our typical wins?
- Should we bid on this one?

### Competitive intelligence
- Run a full competitive intelligence analysis
- Who are we up against on Defence Cyber Uplift?
- What's our competitive positioning on this opportunity?

### **Relationship & risk intelligence** *(elevated value)*
- What's the competitor impact on our Defence tender? *(trigger: "competitor" + "impact")*
- Are any of my current customers doing RFT or EOI this month?
- Which of our existing clients have open tenders we should be aware of?
- Who in our network has access to the procurement team at State Health Authority?
- Has our relationship strength changed with National Infrastructure Corp?

### **Market & pipeline intelligence** *(elevated value)*
- What's the pipeline for Department of Digital Services over the next quarter?
- Are we missing any opportunities from buyers we've won with before?
- Which opportunities align with our Healthcare Data Platform case study?
- What tenders are due in the next 30 days that match our capabilities?

### Strategy & recommendations
- What's our bid-no-bid recommendation for Defence Cyber Uplift?
- Should we pursue this directly or with a partner?
- What's the organisational complexity at National Infrastructure Corp?

## Notes for Future AI Engine

These prompts represent high-value intents to support when building the real bid AI:

1. **M&A / competitor acquisition risk** — Cross-reference competitors, suppliers, and tender relationships
2. **Current customer pipeline** — Join relationship data with opportunity data (RFT/EOI/RFP)
3. **Relationship strength changes** — Track relationship signals over time
4. **Pipeline forecasting** — Buyer-level pipeline and due dates
5. **Case study matching** — Map opportunities to past wins and case studies
